About me

I think of myself as an all-round person, always trying out things in various fields. I have been into different kinds of sports since my school days, commuting to compete in competitions from one part of India to another. It made me understand my passion for traveling. I'm intrigued by the aesthetic views of mountains, valleys, plains while traveling long distances by train. I have explored almost all the northern states and have been to the eastern, southern, and western states of India as well.

I'm an engineer, a programmer and I love structure, order, quality, and consistency. I like working in a team that helps not just me but everyone around me to grow. Learning becomes quicker. Somebody rightly said, "Two heads are better than one!". My friends think of me as a diligent, persistent, focused, hard-working, disciplined person (LOL) and whatnot. However, never go by their words. Contact me and see it for yourself.

I enjoy spending time with individuals coming from various cultures and backgrounds, chatting about sports, TV Shows, games, comics, just to name a few. I don't shy away from watching shows coming from various regions. I'm an avid watcher of Movies, Dramas, and TV Series originating from Asian, European countries, and the USA (of-course Hollywood!). I've recently ventured into the cooking domain. Making new dishes, which most of the time, don't turn out great, but when it does. Squisito!

I am pleased with what I have accomplished so far and intend to bring discoveries and experiences to my life art.
